# Pilot Session Summary

As a group, we came together on June 16th to discuss and share our views on how the annotation process should look. To this end, some of us had prepared some annotated scenarios with distractors and we went over them to discuss general patterns and consistency regarding: choosing the most relevant distractor goal, identifying the best turn to jump in, how to label certain agent responses and overall agent performance, and the double-annotation process. We seemed to be on one line about most things and created 3 duos, each responsible for their own 3 domains. The goal was to create the annotations, double-annotate one scenario from your duo to reach a lower bound for the double annotation rate of 20%, possibly create more scenarios if time allows it, and come together to discuss overall findings of everyone and how we can write it up as a nice comparison and analysis part.
